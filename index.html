Problem

Lately, we have noticed a mutual problem at people with no common background: the chaos of everyday life. Many often complain about the amount of daily tasks they have so they postpone them until the last moment. Postponing can lead to more stress and therefore have a negative impact on one's health. When people begin to be conscious about it, they turn to apps to declutter their life. These apps consist of to do lists, notes, trackers etc, but the main issue is that there isn't an app which includes them all keeping up with more than one app can be time and energy consuming.

Solution

The solution we offer is a mobile application which lets you track your habits, tasks, spendings, moods and prepares monthly reports. The purpose of these reports is to evaluate your daily activities and evolution. Besides that, the user can benefit from various related articles for further reading.

Customer segments

Our application is intended for everyone looking forward to organize, track and improve their life personally or professionally.

Competition

  • Everyday
    Everyday is an app that lets you track your habits. Each user can add and customize his own habits. The user can also visualize his progress and the app generates statistics regarding each user’s progress.
  • Any.do
    Any.do is an app that helps you stay more organized. The user can create shopping lists, to do lists and he can also prioritize his tasks. The user can set a reminder for each event added to the calendar.
  • Daylio
    Daylio is an app where you can have a journal without having to write your thoughts. The user can pick the mood of the day and he can also add the activities he’s done during the day. Based on the user’s input the app generates statistics.

Our advantage

If a person wants to keep a journal, keep track of his habits, insert his daily activities and create a shopping list, he has to download at least 2 or 3 apps in order to do so. Track it! has all of these features integrated and it is designed to be that app that makes life easier and helps you stay organised and motivated. Our advantage over the competition is that, at the moment, there is no other app that has all of these specifications integrated.

Key metrics

Key metrics are numbers and actions on the mobile application that matter to reach our strategic objectives. Here are a few points that we intend to follow:

  • Number of downloads and installs
  • App acquisition - where the downloads came from
  • Active users - Daily Active Users (DAU) and Monthly Active Users (MAU)
  • Average Daily Sessions Per Daily Active Users - the number of sessions per day divided by the number of DAU
  • Average session length
  • Number of accesses per page/feature
  • Retention Rate - how many users return to the app after using it previously within a set period of time
  • Churn Rate - the opposite of retention rate, how many users stopped using the app after the same set period of time
  • Average revenue per user (ARPU) - the total revenue divided by the number of subscriptions
  • Cost per acquisition (CPA) - how much acquiring new users costs
  • Return on investment (ROI) - measures the gain or loss generated by the money invested versus the money spent building the app
  • Rating and reviews given by users

Cost structure

Buying an already developed application can imply an very high cost for a startup, considering a rough estimation provided by a simple site like howmuchtomakeanapp.com an application containing the features needed by trackIT can cost as much as 30.000$. The application development is not a kind of task that can be avoided for the aims of this kind of business for reducing such a high price we can take advantage of our own team programming experience. In the beginning we could launch a beta version of an application and test it on a small group of target users which can provide us some feedback.

After that we can take into consideration that the cost of publishing an application on Google Play is 25$ one time fee and in case we consider extending the market the Apple App Store will add an 99$ annual developer fee. However, if we develop an application that can be accessed through browser the hosting cost for the domain name would be a monthly fee that depends on the name chosen.

Revenue streams

the app will be available for free download but will also include a paid subscription for extended features. The app will offer 3 choices:

  • Free subscription which will include a limited use of journal and activity tracking features
  • Pro subscription which will add more features for tracking habits
  • Custom subscription which will offer users an extended use by giving suggestions based on their behaviour and analysis of all tracked activities, all of this with their individual consent
