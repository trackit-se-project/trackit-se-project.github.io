Market research

Size of target market

Our main target market is: young adults (20 - 40 years old) or any other person that needs to keep track of different aspects in general as well. The population with these age range in Romania is about 40% of the total number, meaning 7.8 millions of people. Among these, about 50% live in the urban area and only 80% have a smartphone, leaving us a total of 3.12 millions of people.

Assuming that only 30% will try the application and that only 40% of them will actually purchase the application after the trial, then the market size is:
(30 / 100) * (40 / 100) * Total number of target market * Price of application

Potentian market share over a period of 5 years

A company's market share is its sales measured as a percentage of an industry's total revenues. You can determine a company's market share by dividing its total sales or revenues by the industry's total sales over a fiscal period.

For estimation, we looked at some of our competitors’ revenue in October 2019. We estimated that that is their revenue for every month of this year.

  • Bullet Journal - 5k dollars revenue for October 2019 which means 60k dollars in 2019
  • Everyday - 5k dollars revenue for October 2019 which means 60k dollars in 2019
  • Laviolet Bullet Journal - 5k dollars revenue for October 2019 which means 60k dollars in 2019
  • Journal it! - 9k dollars revenue for October 2019 which means 108k dollars in 2019
  • Dreamie Planner - 5k dollars revenue for October 2019 which means 60k dollars in 2019
  • Daylio - 40k dollars revenue for October 2019 which means 480k dollars in 2019

Since the majority of people become more and more focused on time management and self development, we assumed that the revenue will increase over the years.

Year 1: Total sales of the industry: 828k dollars. Our estimated sales: 30k dollars. That means that the market share in our first year is equal to 0.03 or 3%.

Year 2: Total sales of the industry: 900k dollars. Our estimated sales: 80k dollars. That means that the market share in our first year is equal to 0.08 or 8%.

Year 3: Total sales of the industry: 1m dollars. Our estimated sales: 110k dollars. That means that the market share in our first year is equal to 0.11 or 11%.

Year 4: Total sales of the industry: 1.5m dollars. Our estimated sales: 200k dollars. That means that the market share in our first year is equal to 0.13 or 13%.

Year 5: Total sales of the industry: 2m dollars. Our estimated sales: 300k dollars. That means that the market share in our first year is equal to 0.15 or 15%.
