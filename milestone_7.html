Minimum Viable Product

Considering the main idea of this project is to create a big app to include a lot more functionalities than the existing habit tracking apps. As said before this app wants to offer a variety of options for users to track habits and improve overall performance.

Building a MVP having enough features so that users can have a good initial experience meant identifying the most important functionalities. After all the feedback we received from our previous discussions with potential users as well as from the coordinators, we established the key aspects of our app. Besides helping organizing daily tasks the most important purpose of this app is to increase productivity and improve personal health and life-style. Therefore, in order to deal up with the competitors, out of all the components we want this app to deliver, we considered to be the most useful for our customers the following features:

  • To do list
  • Calendar
  • Notes
  • Sleep and water tracker

After the validation did on previously milestones we hope early users will have a clear vision of what this application stands to be: an all in one app to help you be productive and live a healthy life.

To do lists are a key solution for users who want to keep up with everything they need to accomplish in a day. Having it synchronized with the calendar makes it easier to stay up to date to all events, meetings and birthdays throughout a month.

Notes offers the alternative to organize all the ideas and thoughts so you don’t miss anything.

As the main characteristics that aim to increase health and lifestyle, water and sleep trackers, are the ones with the biggest impact on the users. Logging the amount of drank water and number of hours slept can give users the benefit of an improved health and decrease stress level.
